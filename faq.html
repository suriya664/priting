3D printing, also known as additive manufacturing, is a process that creates three-dimensional objects by building up layers of material based on a digital model. We use various technologies including FDM, SLA, SLS, and metal printing to transform your digital designs into physical objects.
Standard orders typically take 24-48 hours for printing plus shipping time. Express options are available for urgent projects with same-day or next-day printing. The exact time depends on the complexity, size, and material of your parts.
We accept STL, OBJ, STEP, STP, and 3MF files. For best results, we recommend STL files for FDM printing, STEP files for complex assemblies, and OBJ files for detailed models. Maximum file size is 50MB.
Ensure your model is a watertight mesh with no holes, has proper wall thickness (minimum 0.8mm for FDM), and is oriented correctly for printing. Our team will review your files and notify you of any issues before printing.
Pricing is based on material volume, material type, print quality, quantity, and any post-processing services. Use our instant quote calculator on the pricing page to get an accurate estimate for your project.

We offer standard shipping (3-5 business days), express shipping (1-2 business days), and international shipping. All orders include real-time tracking and insurance.
Yes, we ship worldwide. International shipping times vary by destination but typically range from 5-15 business days. Customs fees and import duties may apply depending on your country.
